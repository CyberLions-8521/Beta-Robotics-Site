Who Are The CyberLions?

Team 8521 CyberLions is a high school robotics team residing at Westminster High School in the city of Westminster, California and founded in August of 2020. The team’s current leaders will utilize their experience to lead many other students in gaining hands-on experiences in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) fields.

Furthermore, our robotics team strives to gather students from grade levels 9-12 to innovate and cultivate well-rounded life skills and experiences including the self-confidence to lead and facilitate others in the future.

What is FIRST

The FIRST Robotics Competition runs from January to April, with approximately 4,000 teams participating globally. Teams compete in districts and regionals to earn a spot at the FIRST Championship. FRC stresses STEAM inspirations by building relationships between students within a subject.

The FIRST Robotics Competition is the main season for CyberLions. All members participate in FRC and the organization is as it appears on the chart. By participating in FRC, we inspire and educate our students in preparation for high-level competition.

“The mission of Team 8521 CyberLions is to provide academic opportunities unique to most high schools and strengthen the collaboration, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills of our members by successfully competing in international competitions.”

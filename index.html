Who Are We?

Team 8521 is a student-led non-profit organization founded in August 2020 and based in Westminster California. Consisting of over 40 highschool students who share the same passion and intrest in Robotics, we showcase our leadership and innovation skills through participating in the FIRST Robotics Competition.
